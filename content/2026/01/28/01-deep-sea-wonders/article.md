# Deep Sea Wonders
By Karmen Hardy

The deep sea holds some of the most alien-looking creatures on Earth. From glowing jellyfish to massive squids, the bioluminescent dance in the dark is a sight to behold.

## The Midnight Zone
At depths where sunlight never reaches, life finds a way to thrive. Many creatures produce their own light for communication, camouflage, and attracting prey.
