# Colorful Reefs
By Karmen Hardy

Coral reefs are the rainforests of the sea. They support an incredible diversity of life, from the smallest cleaner shrimp to the largest reef sharks.

## Ecosystem Health
The vibrancy of a reef is a direct indicator of its health. Protecting these fragile structures is crucial for marine biodiversity and coastal protection.
