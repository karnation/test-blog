# The Mysterious Abyss
By Karmen Hardy

The Mariana Trench remains one of the least explored places on our planet. Pressure levels are crushing, yet even here, life persists in small, specialized forms.

## High Pressure Life
Creatures at these depths have unique adaptations to survive the immense pressure, often lacking rigid structures or air pockets.
